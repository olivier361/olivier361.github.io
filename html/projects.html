Past Programming Projects & Previously Released Games

Carnet de Jeux (French Educational Games):

Uncover one of Victoria's most important historical figures for the french community in this interactive online game for kids.

This game developed exclusively for the Société Francophone de Victoria has for goal of helping children aged 6 to 11 years old learn french as well as discovering about the history of some important figures that helped form the french community in Victoria throughout centuries.

We encourage parents and teachers to play along this approximately twenty minute long game with their kids or students as you will go through puzzles, enigmas and questions to unlock clues to uncover a mysterious historical figure.

*Myself and the Société Francophone de Victoria would like to thank Canadian Heritage for helping fund this project.
